## Valid versus complete

A document can be **authentic** yet not tell the full story. For example, an allocation letter may be real while the pathway from community land to the current seller is unclear. Buyers often confuse “looks official” with “fully explained.”

## Common blind spots

- Names on the plan that do not match the seller’s story  
- Descriptions that do not match the access road you were shown  
- Pressure to pay before you receive a coherent bundle of related papers  

## What a verification report adds

We summarise **what we saw**, **what aligned**, and **where questions remain**, in plain language. That gives you a checklist for your lawyer and a calmer way to negotiate—or walk away.

## The proxy problem

Relatives and agents mean well, but they are not always trained to read survey language, check plan consistency, or push back when a story changes between calls. Distance makes **trust default to relationships**, not evidence.

## What “being on the ground” really means

Ground truth is not only a site visit. It includes **maps, coordinates, landmarks**, and how the seller describes the plot compared to what documents say. When those layers disagree, that is information—not noise.
